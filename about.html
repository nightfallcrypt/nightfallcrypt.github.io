About

Where the darkness has a voice.

Nightfall Crypt is an original horror storytelling channel dedicated to one thing: stories that stay with you. Not jump scares. Not cheap tricks. Just carefully crafted horror that gets under your skin and refuses to leave.

The Stories

Every story published on Nightfall Crypt is an original work — written from scratch, polished, and narrated with intention. We cover psychological horror, supernatural dread, domestic terror, and everything in between.

New stories drop every Wednesday and Friday at 8 PM CT. Each release includes a full narrated video, a prelude, and teaser shorts.

The Schedule

Two stories per week, every week. Wednesday and Friday at 8 PM CT on YouTube and Facebook. Teasers and preludes roll out earlier in the week to build anticipation.
