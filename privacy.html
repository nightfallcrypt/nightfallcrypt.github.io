Privacy Policy

How Nightfall Crypt handles data in the NightfallCrypt Video Manager.

Nightfall Crypt (“we”, “us”) operates the NightfallCrypt Video Manager application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection and use of personal data.

Data We Collect

This application is a private content publishing tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from third-party users. The application operates solely on behalf of the Nightfall Crypt channel owner.

Third-Party Services

We use TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook APIs solely to publish content to our own accounts. Each platform’s own privacy policy governs the data they collect. We do not receive or retain personal information about viewers or followers through these integrations.

OAuth Tokens

Access tokens obtained through TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook OAuth flows are stored locally and used exclusively to authenticate publishing requests. They are never shared with third parties.

Cookies

This application does not use cookies or any client-side tracking technology.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
